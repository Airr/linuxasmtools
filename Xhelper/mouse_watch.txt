
PURPOSE
-------

mouse_watch shows shows the location of left
mouse clicks.  It is intended to assist xhelper
by giving x/y positions for the ^click_left
command.

USAGE
-----

mouse_click sends its output to stdout and
should be run from a terminal.  To capture
click information in a file use:

  mouse_click > log

To watch the click activity, move terminal
to a unused area or screen and type:

  mouse_click

To exit mouse_click, press the right mouse
button.
